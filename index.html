Welcome to my page!

My name is Tomáš Novotný and I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Logic and Theory of Discrete Systems at RWTH Aachen University. My research topic is graph similarity, graph isomorphism, and mathematical logic.

I obtained a M.Sc. degree at RWTH Aachen University. My master's thesis aims to improve the understanding of lower bounds on the iteration number of the Weisfeiler-Leman algorithm, which is used as a heuristic for the graph isomorphism problem.

I have finished my Bachelor's degree at Faculty of Informatics at Masaryk University in Brno (see my MUNI profile) and my Bachelor thesis was concerned about zero-knowledge proof systems using cycles of elliptic curves. The thesis was awarded by the Dean's Award [2021] for an outstanding final thesis.

I am a passionate musician, a speedcuber, and an all-time language learner.
